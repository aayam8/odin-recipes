Wai Wai Sandeko

Wai Wai Sandheko is a tasty and filling snack that can be made in a jiffy. The snack can be made using simple ingredients which will be explained in this article. Before making this snack we will need following equipments to prepare the snack:

  • Mixing Bowl
  • Plastic Gloves
  • Kitchen Knife
Now let us look at the ingredients required to prepare this tasty snack. The ingredients listed here are for a single serving,
  1. Wai-Wai Instant Noodles
  2. One medium Onion
  3. One large Tomato
  4. Two green chillies
  5. Two table spoon tomato ketchup
  6. One table spoon vegetable oil
  7. Three table spoon lemon juice

Instructions

Take a mixing bowl. Open the instant noodle packet and sperate the seasoning packets from the noodle. Crush the noodles and transfer it into the mixing bowl. Now, chop the onion, tomato and green chilles and add it to the crushed noodles. Now, cut open the seasoning packets and add it to the mix. Then add two table spoons of tomato ketchup, one table spoon of vegetable oil and three table spoon of lemon juice. Now, wear the plastic gloves and mix all the ingredients throughly. Add salt to the mix if you find the taste bland.
After mixing has been done, take a serving bowl and transfer the contents. Enjoy the snack !!!!
